WHo are surveyors?

  • Surveyors are professionals who assess the loss or damage and serve as a link between the insurer and the insured. They usually function only in non life business. Their job is to assess the actual loss and avoid false claims.

How can anyone be a surveyor?

  • Any person wishing to become a surveyor must pass a NISA exam, and get himself categorized as per Beema Samiti norms. There are 3 categories of surveyor A,B,C. The categories are being awarded by Beema samiti depending upon the experience.
